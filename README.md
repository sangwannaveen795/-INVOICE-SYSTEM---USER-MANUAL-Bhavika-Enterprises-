HOW TO OPEN THE SOFTWARE:
-------------------------
1. Locate the file named "invoice.html" on your computer or phone.
2. Double-click it. It will open in your default web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, etc.).
3. You do not need internet to use this; it works offline.

KEY FEATURES:
-------------
1. Automatic Calculations: The Grand Total updates automatically when you change quantity or price.
2. Save System: You can save customer invoices to a list.
3. Print Ready: Clicking "Print Invoice" removes buttons and menus for a clean paper print.
4. Contact Info: Includes your Phone, WhatsApp, and Payment details (Paytm/UPI).

-------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO USE:
-------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1: FILL CUSTOMER DETAILS
   - Enter the "Customer Name" in the top right box.
   - The "Date" is automatically set to today, but you can change it.
   - The "Invoice No" is generated automatically.

STEP 2: ADD ITEMS
   - Type the "Item Name" (e.g., Stone Truck).
   - Enter Quantity (Qty) and Price.
   - The Total for that row is calculated instantly.
   - Click "+ Add Item" to add more rows.
   - Click "X" to delete a row.

STEP 3: SAVE THE INVOICE (DATABASE)
   - Once you are done, click the green "üíæ Save Invoice" button at the top.
   - You will see a success message.
   - This saves the invoice in your browser's memory.

STEP 4: VIEW OLD INVOICES
   - Click the grey "üìÇ View Saved Invoices" button.
   - A panel will open showing a list of all past customers.
   - Click "Load" to bring an old invoice back onto the screen to print it again.
   - Click "Delete" to remove it permanently.

STEP 5: PRINTING
   - Click the blue "Print Invoice" button at the bottom.
   - The print preview window will open.
   - You can save it as a "PDF" or select your printer to print on paper.
   - Note: The background colors (Blue header/Orange text) are set to print automatically.
